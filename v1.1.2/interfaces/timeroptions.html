duration

duration: TimerDuration
  • Defined in types.ts:194

The maximum duration for the timer in milliseconds. Once the time has reached the duration, the timer will stop. If "infinite", the timer will never stop on its own.

Default: "infinite"

updateInterval

updateInterval: number
  • Defined in types.ts:203

The delay in milliseconds between every update. Time itself is always calculated with Date, this is just how often it recalculates and calls all subscriber functions.

Default: 100
